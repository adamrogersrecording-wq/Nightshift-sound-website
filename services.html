Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 48 hours for most projects. Monthly package clients get priority processing. Rush delivery (24 hours) is available for +$50.

Do you really offer unlimited revisions?

Yes! We'll keep refining until you're completely satisfied. Most clients are happy after 1-2 rounds, but we're here for however many you need.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept WAV, MP3, AIFF, M4A, and most common audio formats. For best quality, send uncompressed WAV or AIFF files.

Can you work with video files?

Absolutely! For YouTube projects, you can send video files and we'll extract, process, and resync the audio perfectly.

What if my recording has major issues?

Send us a sample first! We'll assess it for free and let you know if it's salvageable and what it would cost to fix.

Do monthly packages roll over?

Unused episodes don't automatically roll over, but we're flexible! If you need to skip a week or adjust your package, just let us know.
